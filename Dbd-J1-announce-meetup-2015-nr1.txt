"Dive into the Job Interview (Part 1)" German Pro-Workshop :: Bewerbungsstart 1

EN: "You need to speak German fluently to find a proper job in Berlin."(*)

The "Get a Better Job in Berlin" series of workshops is made for expats searching for a better job or internship in a German-speaking company. These unique training sessions combine language training by a professional trainer with hands-on activities, self-study tools and networking opportunities in German. Your professional German trainer, Christian Reuter, will help you improve your job opportunities. Language support is offered in English and French as needed.

(*) That's what an expat said on http://www.settle-in-berlin.com/find-a-job-in-berlin/
